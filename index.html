Covid-19

Most common symptoms
Mild to heavy fever.
Dry cough.
Tiredness.

Serious symptoms
Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath.
Chest pain or pressure.
Loss of speech or movement.

Preventions
Wear a face cover.
Wash your hands.
Keep a safe distance.

Tuberculosis

Symptoms
Coughing up blood.
Chest Pain.
Loss of appetite.
Feeling tired all the time.

Preventions
Get BCG vaccination.
Maintain proper weight as per BMI.
Check for HIV infection.
control diabetes.
Always cover your mouth when you cough or sneeze.
Keep all your doctor appointments.
Don't visit other people and don't invite them to visit you.

Diabetes Mellitus

Symptoms
Excessive thirst.
Extreme hunger.
Very dry skin.
Unexplained weight loss.

Preventions
Exercise regularly.
Maintain proper Diet.
Add more fiber.
Control Blood Sugar.
Following a healthy eating plan.
Talk to your health care provider.
Losing weight and keeping it off.

Do's and Dont's:

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19)

COVID-19 affects different people in different ways. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization.
How to deal with covid-19

Do's

1. Washing hands frequently
2. Cover Your Mouth and Nose
3. Consult A Doctor If Sick
4.Take vitamin-C enrich diet
5. Sanitize your hands before entering home

Dont's

1. Avoid Close Contact With Anyone
2. Do Not Spit
3. Avoid Using Public Transport
3. Do Not Use Over The Counter Medicines
4. Don’t Touch Your Face
5. Don’t Panic, Take It Easy

Do's and Dont's:

Precautions During Pregnancy

First Trimester

Do's

1. Change your current exercise routine.
2. Eat a balanced diet.(fruits, vegetables,proteins,fibres)
3. Take folic acid and vitamin suppliments.

Dont's

1. Avoid exercise that puts any pressure on abdominal area.
2. Avoid unripe papaya
3. Dont take too much stress

Second Trimester

Do's

1. Ensure your diet is rich in iron.
2. Get plenty of vitamin C.
3. Enrol in Yoga Class.

Dont's

1. Avoid junk food.
2. Never miss an appointment of doctor
3. Dont take too much stress.

Third Trimester

Do's

1. Avoid Exercise.
2. Avoid Wrong sleeping position.
3. Eat enough and healthy.

Dont's

1. Do not go under stressful situations.
2. Avoid long travelling.
